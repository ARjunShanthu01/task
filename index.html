Salaar: Part 1 - Ceasefire is an upcoming pan-India action thriller film written and directed by Prashanth Neel and produced by Vijay Kiragandur. The magnum opus stars Rebel Star Prabhas in the titular role as 'Salaar' with Shruthi Hassan as 'Aadhya' and Prithviraj Sukumaran as 'Vardharaja Mannaar'.
